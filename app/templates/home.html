Risk Analysis

Takes a user inputed mutual fund and comparable index and populates six graphs. Rolling periods are considered to analyze the risk profiles of periods of different lengths.

401k Calculator

Calculates a pension plan and base 401k from user inputed information. Using market and US bond returns from 1927-2021, each possible 35 year return window is created based on possible portfolio weights. Then, for each of these scenarios the lowest additional employer contribution percent to make average withdrawal equal to pension withdrawal is calculated. For each portfolio weight, the average of all employer contribution percents is calculated. From these calculated averages the average, minimum, and maximum are taken and extrapolated on the graph.
